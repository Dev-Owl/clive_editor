# Code of Conduct

We want CliveEdit to be a respectful, constructive, and welcoming project for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and professional.
- Give feedback that is helpful and specific.
- Assume good intent and discuss disagreements respectfully.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insults, or hostile behavior.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Disruptive conduct in issues, pull requests, discussions, or other project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainer at `christian.muehle@devowl.de`.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or moderate contributions and participation that do not follow this code of conduct.
